About Dermatologist AI Assistant

Dermatologist AI Assistant is an intelligent skin-care support application designed to help users better understand their skin using the power of Artificial Intelligence.

The app combines advanced image analysis and skin-science knowledge to provide quick, easy, and informative skin insights.

The application offers skin disease detection by analyzing skin images and identifying possible conditions such as acne, eczema, or other skin concerns. Based on individual skin type and detected issues, it generates a personalized skin-care routine to promote healthy and well-maintained skin.

Dermatologist AI Assistant also includes ingredient and product analysis, helping users understand what skincare ingredients do, whether they are safe, and how suitable they are for different skin types.

In addition, the app can explain dermatoscopy reports in simple language, making medical skin reports easier to understand for non-experts.

This application is built to support awareness, early understanding, and informed decision-making, but it does not replace a certified dermatologist.

It aims to make skincare knowledge accessible, simple, and user-friendly for everyone.

Built for awareness, not diagnosis.
